<p><ul><li>PC-Based Vision System</li><li>Smart Camera-Based Vision System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Machine Vision System market is primarily divided into two types: PC-Based Vision Systems and Smart Camera-Based Vision Systems. PC-Based Vision Systems utilize powerful computers for image processing and analysis, offering extensive capabilities for complex tasks and high-resolution imaging. In contrast, Smart Camera-Based Vision Systems integrate processing capabilities directly into the camera, providing compact solutions suitable for simpler applications and environments where space is limited. Both systems play crucial roles in automation, quality control, and improved operational efficiency in industrial processes.</p></p>
