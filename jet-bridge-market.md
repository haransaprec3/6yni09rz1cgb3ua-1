<p><strong>The Jet Bridge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Apron drive jet bridge</li><li>Nose-loader jet bridge</li><li>Dual jet bridge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Jet bridges are crucial for airport operations, facilitating passenger movement between terminals and aircraft. The apron drive jet bridge features a flexible structure allowing it to reach various aircraft sizes, typically driven by an apron-based mechanism. Nose-loader jet bridges connect directly to aircraft nose sections, ideal for narrow-bodied planes, maximizing space efficiency. Dual jet bridges accommodate two aircraft simultaneously, enhancing throughput and reducing turnaround times, making them essential for busy airports aiming for operational efficiency and improved passenger experience.</p></p>
